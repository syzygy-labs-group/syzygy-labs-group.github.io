Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about EDI Nexus, ERP implementation, and integration services.

What does Syzygy Labs provide besides ERP services?

In addition to ERP implementation and customization, we provide EDI Nexus, a cloud EDI hub SaaS, plus custom development, business intelligence, and integration services to connect your full operations stack.

How do I get started with EDI Nexus or ERP implementation?

Start with a consultation call. We assess your current workflows, identify whether EDI Nexus, ERP work, or both are the right fit, and then provide a clear implementation plan with milestones and onboarding steps.

Can EDI Nexus and your ERP solutions be customized to our workflows?

Yes. We support configurable workflows and integrations in EDI Nexus and provide deep ERP customization so your process, data structure, and reporting requirements are reflected in the final solution.

What kind of support do you offer after implementation?

We provide post-launch support including troubleshooting, enhancements, and operational guidance to keep your EDI and ERP workflows running smoothly as requirements evolve.

Is EDI Nexus cloud-based?

Yes. EDI Nexus is a cloud-based EDI hub SaaS that gives your team secure access without managing on-prem infrastructure, while enabling faster partner onboarding and easier scaling.

How secure are your EDI and ERP solutions?

Security is built into our delivery approach, including strong access controls, encryption, and operational safeguards to protect sensitive business and transaction data.

How long does implementation usually take?

Timelines vary by scope. EDI Nexus onboarding can be fast for standard workflows, while ERP and custom integration projects usually take longer based on complexity, data migration, and testing requirements.
